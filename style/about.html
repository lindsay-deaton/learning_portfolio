Lindsay Deaton

It all started back when MySpace was, what seemed to be, the only social networking platform. I used to sit all day after school editing my site. The style adds included colors, images, different types of font styles, music, pictures, and sections holding everything in their places. That was 15 years ago and since then I had forgotten what enjoyment I got out of it, until now. At one point, out of high school, I entertained the teachings of information technology just thinking, 'I'm good at this whole computers thing.. During that short journey, I realized I'd be one of very few women in the industry and at the time, my career in sales was taking off. Many years later, here I am, realizing I should have taken the path to support what I knew would be the future and what better avenue than one that came somewhat easy to me?
